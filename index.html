Marwan Meqbil is an IT Specialist, originally from the West Bank. Marwan obtained his B.A. Information Technology degree at 2012. My professional experience includes facilitating peace-building processes, translation and interpretation in Arabic, English, and Hebrew. I had the passion to communicate with people from other cultures.

About Me

Marwan’s encounter with other cultures began at an early age, when he volunteered in summer camps organized by international organizations in the refugee camp aimed at understanding Palestinian culture and building bridges among people of different backgrounds. In 2010, Marwan gained a leadership position in organizing these summer camps. His primary responsibilities included organizing sightseeing and archeological tours, liaison with local and international organizations, organizing cultural events and serving as a focal point for similar activities in the area of Bethlehem. Furthermore, Marwan was a mentor for a group of 15 other volunteers in his camp. Throughout his higher education, Marwan kept his social contribution going and also started to support himself and his family by working with tourists and helping his father at his family small building materials shop.
